# 经济商誉与EBITDA的陷阱
## 摘要
会计商誉被逐年摊销减少，但经济商誉实际上在不断增长。喜诗糖果的案例完美展示了两者的背离：账面商誉从3600万降到2100万，但税前利润从2700万增长到5900万。
## 核心观点
1. GAAP要求商誉在不超过40年内摊销，但会计上商誉的消失绝不意味着经济商誉的减少——事实上往往相反
2. 喜诗糖果案例：1983年用1100万净运营资产赚2700万税前利润；1997年用500万赚5900万——经济商誉戏剧性增长，远超账面减少
3. GEICO的16亿美元会计商誉将每年被冲减4000万直到2036年，但其经济商誉很可能大幅增加
4. 关键警告：折旧与商誉摊销完全不同——折旧是真实经济成本，与工资、材料、税一样真实
5. EBITDA（息税折旧摊销前利润）不是有意义的业绩衡量指标——忽略折旧重要性的管理层容易做出错误决策
## 原文引用
> "The economic facts could not be more different. In 1983, See's earned about $27 million pre-tax on $11 million of net operating assets; in 1997 it earned $59 million on $5 million of net operating assets. Clearly See's economic goodwill has increased dramatically during the interval rather than decreased."

> "Investors are often led astray by CEOs and Wall Street analysts who equate depreciation charges with the amortization charges we have just discussed. In no way are the two the same: With rare exceptions, depreciation is an economic cost every bit as real as wages, materials, or taxes."

> "We do not think so-called EBITDA is a meaningful measure of performance. Managements that dismiss the importance of depreciation — and emphasize 'cash flow' or EBITDA — are apt to make faulty decisions."
