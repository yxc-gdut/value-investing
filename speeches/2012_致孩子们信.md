Dear Susie, Howie and Peter:
It’s been six years since my pledge of 17,500,000 Berkshire B shares
(adjusted for a 50 for 1 split) to each of your foundations.
| knew you would apply your considerable brains and energies in order to
make the most of the funds from my gift. However, you have exceeded my high
expectations. Your mother would be as proud of you as |am. | see her influence
in what you are accomplishing.
I’ve decided, therefore, to double the original pledge that now stands at
12,220,852 B shares. On the new base of 24,441,704 pledged shares, | will
distribute 1,222,085 shares to each of the three foundations next July with that
amount decreasing at 5% per year subsequently.
Over time, | would expect the value of the annual distribution to average
more than $100 million, though it will vary substantially from year to year. I’m
confident you will use the money wisely, each in your own way.
Love,
